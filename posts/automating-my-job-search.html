Automating my Job Search

What does the system do?

Cull duplicate postings

Keep a running list of applications statuses

System Outline

One of the main issues is getting new postings to add to the system. All job boards I am aware of have reasonably strong anti-scrapping measures; nothing that can't be worked around, but a pain nonetheless. However, almost all job boards allow email alerts with some or all matchs for a particular search. So, you can setup email alerts and scrap those emails instead of the website itself. I made a completely seperate gmail account solely for this purpose, and I'd recommend doing the same for security reasons.
